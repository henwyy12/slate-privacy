Privacy Policy

Slate — Bill & Credit Tracker

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Overview

Slate is a bill tracking app that helps you manage and pay your bills on time. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

When you use Slate, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information — If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address to create your account.
  • Bill data — Bill names, amounts, due dates, categories, notes, and payment status that you enter into the app.
  • Preferences — Your chosen currency, notification settings, and display preferences.

How We Store Your Data

  • Locally on your device — All bill data is stored on your device first. The app works fully offline.
  • Cloud sync (optional) — If you sign in with Google, your data is synced to our cloud database (hosted on Supabase) so you can access it across devices.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal data to anyone.
  • We do not share your data with third-party advertisers.
  • We do not use tracking or analytics tools to monitor your behavior.
  • We do not store photos or images. If receipt scanning is added in the future, images are processed temporarily and immediately deleted.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In — Used for authentication. Google's privacy policy applies to the sign-in process.
  • Supabase — Used to store synced data securely in the cloud. Data is encrypted in transit.
  • Apple In-App Purchases — Used to process payments for the Pro upgrade. Apple's privacy policy applies to transactions.

Notifications

If you enable bill reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device. We do not use push notification servers or track notification interactions.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete all your data at any time from the Settings page in the app using the "Reset all data" option. This removes all local data and, if you are signed in, all cloud data associated with your account.

Children's Privacy

Slate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at slatedeveloper@gmail.com.
